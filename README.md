# Privacy Policy for SnapDecide

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

## Overview

SnapDecide ("the app", "we", "our") is a decision-making tool that includes a spinning wheel, dice tray, spinning bottle, and finger-tap selector. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

**Short version: SnapDecide does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information, and does not have a server that receives data from your device.

The only data the app stores is:

- **Wheel options and saved wheels** — the labels you type into the spinning wheel (e.g., "Pizza", "Sushi"). These are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage so your options persist between sessions.
- **Player names in The Bottle** — names you enter for the spinning bottle game. These are also saved locally on your device only.
- **App preferences** — settings such as the number of dice or players you last used.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us, to any server, or to any third party.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprinting data.
- We do not use analytics or crash-reporting services.
- We do not display advertisements.
- We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies.
- We do not share any data with third parties.

---

## Third-Party Services

SnapDecide does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

---

## Children's Privacy

SnapDecide does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations worldwide.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.
- You can clear individual wheel options or player names directly within the app.
- We have no ability to access or delete data on your behalf because we never receive it.

---

## International Users

SnapDecide is available worldwide. Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data transfer across borders. This policy is consistent with the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and equivalent privacy laws in other jurisdictions.
